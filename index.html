About Me

Hi, I am Felippe, an oceanographer of 28 years old and I am from S찾o Paulo, Brazil. When I was 19, I moved to Rio Grande, far south of Brazil to study my main course and passion, Oceanography, at the Federal University of Rio Grande - FURG. In this course, we have subjects from the Chemistry, Biology, Geology and Physics Field, I am choosed Physical Oceanography to continue my path, which was the start of my journey into programming and technology.

During my undergrad period, I had the opportuniy to have a Scholarship sponsored by the National Council for Scientific and Technological Development (CNPq) and study for 15 months in Adelaide, Australia. More than an incredible academic moment, it was also a amazing personal and culture experience in my life. I studied subjects related with Environmental Sciences at the University of South Australia - UniSA for two semesters and at the end of my period in Australia, I was awarded with a Summer Scholarship from the Australian Research Council - Centre of Excellence for Climate System Science, when I then moved to Sydney to be a intern during 6 weeks at the Climate Change Reserch Centre, at the University of New South Wales (UNSW).

Back in Brazil, I finished my degree and went back to S찾o Paulo and at the beginning of 2019 I started to work as Data Analyst in a Digital Marketing Counsultancy Agency. It was a challenged to be in such different business area outside of academy, but it was also very important to my personal & profissional development in different skills and to learn important key concepts of a Business Area. After one year, in March 2020 I moved to Rio de Janeiro to my currently work in the Navy Hydrographic Center, in Brazilian Navy, as a Physical Oceanographer & Data Engineer.

Nowadays, in addition to my work, I spend my time studying and learning both technologies and oceanography/environmental sciences... Before the covid-19 and pandemic, I used to run, practice archery and was starting to climb (indoor), but nowadays is just books, Netflix, Amazon Prime and YouTube... And Google Meets with my friends :).
