MatLab

MatLab was my first programming language that I learned. Back some years ago, MatLab was the main language in physical oceanography to proccess and analyze meteo-oceanographic and remote sensing data, so it was natural to learn this language when I started as a intern at the Laboratory of Studies for Ocean and Climate, at my University, FURG. I entered to this lab in middle of 2013, however my code skills in MatLab were developed after 2016, when I started to work and help my colleagues with some remote sensing data. In the beginning of 2017, at my summer internship at the Climate Change Research Centre (UNSW), I started a project working with Sea Level Anomaly and Rossby Waves in the South Atlantic. During the internship, my supervisor gave me some code receipes where I learned how to work with the sea level anomaly data and the Fast Fourier Transformation (in 2D, so 2d-FFT). Along the year of 2017 I continue to work in this project

My first 3 papers published, all as co-author, I had the role to collect and proccess remote sensing data, geographic data and other types of data. You can check the papers and details in here .
